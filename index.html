What is

the medical

mini-mba?



and why?

The Medical Mini-MBA was created to educate medical students on financial literacy.

Facing the problem of tremendous debt to signing a massive line-of-credit is a big adjustment. We dug deeper and found that these changes in financial status happen going into residency and then as an attending physician.

With an increasingly diverse cohort of medical students and trainees, we wanted to prepare students of all socioeconomic statuses so that they may attain financial wellness. Studies have shown that financial stress impairs student performance. We improve student outcomes by helping students achieve financial wellness.
